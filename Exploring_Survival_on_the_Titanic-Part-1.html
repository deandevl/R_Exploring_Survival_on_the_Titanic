1 Introduction

The following analysis is based on the R script presented by Megan L. Risdal on Kaggle along with data descriptions.

Additional analysis using decision trees and randomForest is provided by Titanic: Getting started with R by Trevor Stephens.

The Titantic data originated from the Vanderbilt Biostat Department
